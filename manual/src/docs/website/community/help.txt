[[help,Get Help]]
= Get Help =

Join the mailing list to discuss and get help, or help others on Stackoverflow.

If you found a bug or want to request a new feature, use the <<issue-tracker>>.

== Questions & Answers ==

To post question to and get answers from the Qi4j community, go to
http://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/qi4j[StackOverflow].

== Discussion ==

To discuss with the Qi4j community; browse and subscribe to the mailing list.

* The list is hosted at http://lists.ops4j.org/mailman/listinfo/qi4j-dev
* To subscribe, send an email to qi4j-dev-subscribe@lists.ops4j.org
* To unsubscribe, send an email to qi4j-dev-unsubscribe@lists.ops4j.org
* The mailing list archive can be browsed at http://www.mail-archive.com/qi4j-dev@lists.ops4j.org[Mail Archive] and http://qi4j-dev.23929.n6.nabble.com/[Nabble].
