Community Support
=================

To get information on upcoming Qi4j events, have a look here:

* http://www.qi4j.org/news.html

Get help from the Qi4j open source community; here are some starting points.

* The mailing-list: http://www.mail-archive.com/qi4j-dev@lists.ops4j.org/info.html[archive], http://lists.ops4j.org/mailman/listinfo/qi4j-dev[sign up].
* The http://www.qi4j.org/[web site].

Report a _bug_ or add a _feature request_:

* http://team.ops4j.org/browse/QI

Questions regarding the _documentation:_
The Qi4j Manual is published online with a comment function, please use that to post any questions or comments.
